Creator FAQ

Short answers for creators considering working with BLKRØM — what this is, how it works, and what we expect.

Do I need experience as a creator or model?

Not necessarily. BLKRØM works with both established creators and women at the very beginning. What matters is intention, reliability and a clear sense of your boundaries.

Do I have to create explicit content?

No. The content level is defined together and always stays within your comfort zone. We can work from suggestive to editorial, from anonymous to fully visible — nothing is forced.

How does the collaboration work?

We start with a portfolio and strategy call, define goals, boundaries and capacity. From there we decide on shoots, content structure and rhythm. Everything stays transparent, planned and documented.

Does BLKRØM earn money from my revenue?

BLKRØM works with custom agreements per creator. A revenue share can be part of the collaboration, but is always discussed individually after the portfolio call. Terms are decided privately and only if both sides agree.

What do I need to invest?

Time, consistency and a real willingness to treat your work as a brand, not a side-effect. You show up prepared, honest about your limits and ready to execute. BLKRØM brings structure, visuals and long-term direction.

Is this open to anyone?

No. BLKRØM is selective and works invite-only or by application. This keeps communication focused and ensures we only take on creators we can genuinely support.
