Creator FAQ

Short answers for creators considering working with BLKRØM — what this is, how it works, and what we expect.

Do I need experience as a creator or model?

Not necessarily. What matters is intention, reliability and a clear sense of your boundaries.

Do I have to create explicit content?

No. The content level is defined together and always stays within your comfort zone.

How does the collaboration work?

We start with a portfolio and strategy call, define goals and capacity, then decide on structure and rhythm.

Does BLKRØM earn money from my revenue?

BLKRØM works with custom agreements per creator. Terms are discussed privately after the portfolio call.

What do I need to invest?

Time, consistency and willingness to treat your work as a brand — not a side-effect.

Is this open to anyone?

No. BLKRØM is selective and works invite-only or by application.
